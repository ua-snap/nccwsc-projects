About the Climate Adaptation Science Centers

The USGS National and Regional Climate Adaptation Science Centers (CASCs) is a partnership-driver program that teams scientists with resource managers and local communities. We work within this partnership to help fish, wildlife, waters, and lands across the country adapt to changing conditions.

Funding applicable science & educating the next generation

The CASCs fund projects generating research, data sets, and tools to help natural and cultural resource managers protect natural places & local communities in a changing climate. The CASC network supports students and early-career scientists and managers through a variety of fellowship and training programs.
